Welcome to our page!

Before and After

The situation in Ukraine with homeless animals is critical. Shelters, volunteers and ordinary people who care are doing their best, but this is not enough now. The country is in flames of war. Funds are needed for food, medical treatment and equipment. Every day it becomes more and more difficult, while the number of animals does not decrease. They know and feel, they come on their own, for feeding, for help, and we try to help. Their eyes say “Help me. At least I won`t be hungry today.” We feed, treat and find homes. Therefore, we will be grateful to everyone who will take part in rescuing animals.

We are a group of volunteers in Ukraine, despite all the horrors of the war, we help animals and those who care for them, we provide help not to shelters (!), but to ordinary citizens, old women and not only, those who take care of their pets even with their meager means. We want goodness to spread throughout the world!

Good will definitely triumph! We do everything possible and impossible for this.

Help in the warfare areas

Every week, with volunteers and fighters of the Ukrainian army, we transfer food to homeless animals in the combat area. There the situation is most critical.
